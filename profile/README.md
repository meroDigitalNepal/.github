# meroDigitalNepal

Open-source digital public infrastructure for Nepal.  

## Vision
A Nepal where every citizen can access, understand, and participate in their government, and where digital public services are built to a standard worthy of the people they serve.

## Goals
**Goal 1 — Build Digital Solutions for Nepal**
Nepal's challenges are Nepal's to solve. Whether it’s connecting citizens with their representatives, helping government offices communicate at scale, enabling local communities to access public services, or anything in between; If it moves Nepal forward digitally, MDN builds and ships.

**Goal 2 - Raise the floor on public digital infrastructure**
Every rupee of government money spent on digital services should produce something meaningfully better than the bare minimum. MDN establishes that standard — openly, publicly, and without negotiation. 

**Goal 3 — Build for everyone, from the start**
Digital Nepal should not leave anyone behind. MDN bakes inclusivity into the foundation — accessible design, regional language support, and low-bandwidth considerations built into every component from day one.
